Student Life at Mahindra University

Explore our vibrant student-led organizations and find where you belong.

Enigma

The Official Coding and Software Development Club of Mahindra University.

Enigma is dedicated to fostering a strong culture of competitive coding, open-source contribution, and software engineering. We organize weekly programming contests, hackathons, and software development workshops. Whether you are a beginner writing your first "Hello World" or an experienced developer building complex systems, Enigma provides the resources and community to help you grow. We focus heavily on software, web development, algorithms, and project-based learning.
Weekly sessions Medium commitment

Electrowizards

The Robotics, IoT, and Hardware Engineering Guild.

Electrowizards brings together students interested in electronics, microcontrollers, embedded systems, and robotics. We host practical, hands-on lab sessions working with Arduino, Raspberry Pi, circuits, sensors, and mechanical design. Our club builds projects for national robotics competitions and works on innovative internet-of-things prototypes. Perfect for hardware enthusiasts who love building physical systems!
Bi-weekly sessions High commitment
Website Instagram Email Us

Orators Club

Public Speaking, Literary Arts, and Model United Nations Guild.

Orators Club is the hub for public speaking, debating, poetry, and Model United Nations (MUN) at Mahindra University. We believe in the power of voice and expression. We hold weekly practice debates, Toastmasters-style speeches, and creative writing circles. Join us to build your confidence, refine your persuasion skills, and engage in stimulating intellectual discourse about global issues.
Weekly sessions Low commitment
Instagram Email Us

Magna

The Business, Finance, and Entrepreneurship Club.

Magna is the entrepreneurship and business club at Mahindra University. We help students turn ideas into viable startups, teach financial literacy, and analyze market trends. We hold case study competitions, guest talks from founders, mock stock market trading, and pitch events. If you are passionate about business, leadership, startups, and consulting, Magna is the place to network and build your business acumen.
Monthly sessions Medium commitment
Website Instagram Email Us

Aether

Aviation, Aerospace, and Drone Building Club.

Aether is our aviation and aerospace enthusiast community. We study aerodynamics, build radio-controlled planes, design drones, and experiment with flight simulation. We hold hands-on workshops on drone assembly, pilot safety, and aircraft mechanics. Members also participate in design-build-fly competitions. High commitment but incredibly rewarding for those interested in aerospace and aerodynamics.
Bi-weekly sessions High commitment
Instagram Email Us

Aria

The Music and Performing Arts Club.

Aria is the creative home for musicians, singers, songwriters, and instrumentalists at Mahindra University. We cover everything from classical to rock and electronic music. Aria hosts jam sessions, music workshops, and is responsible for all live performances at major university festivals. Join us to share your passion for music, collaborate on performances, and find bandmates.
Weekly sessions Low commitment
Instagram Email Us

Qubit Club

We introduce students to quantum computing through hands-on workshops, technical sessions, hackathons, and collaborative projects. Members explore quantum algorithms, Qiskit, cloud quantum platforms, and emerging research while building a strong foundation in quantum technologies.

Qubit Club is dedicated to introducing students to quantum computing, quantum algorithms, Qiskit, and cloud quantum platforms. Members participate in technical workshops, R&D projects, hackathons, and research in mathematics, physics, computer science, artificial intelligence, and emerging technologies. Best suited for students interested in CS, AI, Math, Physics, and Research.
Weekly sessions Medium commitment
